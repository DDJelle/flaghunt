## Objective
Find and capture the opposing team’s flag before they capture yours.

## Setting Up
1. Hide your flag at a real-world location.
2. Track the enemy’s flag using distance clues.

## Weapons
Choose from:
- **Baseball Bat** (Beats Net Gun)
- **Net Gun** (Beats Chainsaw)
- **Chainsaw** (Beats Baseball Bat)

## Battles
- When close to an opponent, a battle starts.
- The winner continues, the loser respawns at their base.

## Winning
Find the enemy flag and capture it to win!
